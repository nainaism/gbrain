# Ideas

Pages about original ideas, proposals, and insights.

## What goes here
- 成田さん's original thinking and frameworks
- Novel observations about how the world works
- Strategic insights that could become projects
- Hot takes with reasoning

## What does NOT go here
- Routine operational messages → these don't get saved
- Pure questions without embedded observations
- Ideas that are already active projects → `projects/`

## Slug format
- Lowercase kebab-case describing the idea (e.g., `ambient-work-os.md`)
