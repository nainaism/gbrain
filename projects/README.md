# Projects

Pages about active or completed projects.

## What goes here
- Projects with goals, tasks, and deadlines
- Both ai-factory tracked projects and external ones
- Side projects and experiments

## What does NOT go here
- Project management methodology → `concepts/`
- People → `people/`
- Ideas that aren't yet projects → `ideas/`

## Slug format
- Lowercase kebab-case (e.g., `blog-support-tool.md`, `gbrain-integration.md`)
